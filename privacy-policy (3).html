Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Data Collection

This app does not collect personal data. Exchange rates are fetched from third-party APIs (exchangerate-api.com, open.er-api.com). Your conversion history stays on your device.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage to cache exchange rates for offline use and to store your premium status. No tracking cookies are used by this app directly.

This app displays ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.
